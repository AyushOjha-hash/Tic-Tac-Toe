✦ Tic-Tac-Toe

Objective: Be the first to place 3 of your marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.

Turns: X always goes first, then players alternate. Click any empty cell to claim it.

Winning: Three in a row — across, down, or diagonally — wins the round.

Draw: If all 9 cells are filled with no winner, the round is a draw.
